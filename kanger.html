Kanger Valley National Park

Kanger Valley National Park is one of the mosgt beautiful national park in Chhattisgarh. It is located near jagdalpur in the bastar region. The park is famous for its dense forest, wildlife, waterfalls, and limestone caves.

Wildlife

Animals found in the park: -Leopard -Deer -Wild bear -Monkeys -Many Birds -Reptiles

There are many attractive resorts for view.
