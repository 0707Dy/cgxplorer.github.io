sirpur heritage

Sirpur is one of the most important historical and archaeological sites in chhattisgarh.

It is located on the banks of the mahanadi river and was once a great center of religion,culture,and education. it is famous for its ancient temples , monasteries, and archaeological remains.

There are major attractions such as Lakshmana Temple, Surang Tila, Gandheswar Temple. There are many attractive resorts for view.
